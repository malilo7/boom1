ERP Systems

Small Business: TZS 50M-150M | Medium Enterprise: TZS 200M-500M

Custom ERP solutions include inventory, HR, finance modules tailored for Tanzanian regulations (TRA compliance, local currency). Implementation includes:

  4. Testing: QA, security audits, user acceptance
  5. Deployment: Go-live with training & documentation
  6. Maintenance: 3-12 months support included
